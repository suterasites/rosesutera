About

Hi, I'm Rose.

My journey began in school, competing in netball and softball, where I first learned how powerful good nutrition and exercise can be. These days I cycle, run, and train with weights, and I love helping other people feel that same sense of capability in their own bodies.

I believe you are what you eat, but I also believe in balance. Life's too short to deny yourself the occasional treat. Real wellness is the sweet spot between nourishing food and consistent movement, and that's the space I work in with my clients.

What I help with

Four areas where small changes make the biggest difference.

01

Weight loss

Sustainable change built around the way you actually live. No crash diets, no guilt, no off-the-shelf meal plans.

02

Gut health

Bloating, irregularity, food sensitivities. We unpack what's driving it and rebuild a way of eating your gut can settle into.

03

Hormonal health
